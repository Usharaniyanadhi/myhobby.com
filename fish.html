tawa fish fry



step-1

Take all your ingredients for marinating fish. You need chilli powder, turmeric, coriander, garam masala powder, black pepper powder, ginger garlic paste, curry leaves, coriander leaves, lemon juice and salt.
I like to use a mix of kashmiri chilli powder and regular chilli powder. kashmiri chilli powder adds colour.

step-2

Mix all the ingredients given for marination together. I like to use a plate or a shallow bowl for mixing the marination.


step-3

Add in lemon juice. Sometimes I add vinegar to the marination. It adds a tangy taste to the marination which is essential for fried fish.


step-4

Mix the marination together with your hands. Make sure the marination doesn't turn too runny. You want it to be thick.


step-5

Mix the marination together with your hands. Make sure the marination doesn't turn too runny. You want it to be thick.


step-6

Add some water or more lemon juice to your marination to adjust the consistency.


step-7

Now the marination masala is ready. It has to be thick like paste.


step-8

Take your fish pieces. Since this is a whole fish, make deep slashes on the fish. The marination will penetrate through these gashes. You can use any fish like king fish steaks, seer fish or even anchovies. step-9
Apply the marination all over the fish. Apply evenly into the slashes.


step-10

Let the fish marinate for minimum 30 minutes to 1 hour.


step-11

Make sure you apply the marination inside the fish as well.


step-12

Once you apply the marination all over it. You can allow it to marinate for minimum 15 minutes to 1 hour.


step-13

I prefer to fry the fish in coconut oil. Take a shallow frying pan, like cast iron or nonstick. Place fish in hot coconut oil and fry on high heat for 4 to 5 minutes.


step-14

Now flip the fish carefully, reduce the flame and cook on other side for 3 to 4minutes.


step-15

Remove the fish carefully to serving plate. Garnish with coriander leaves, lemon wedges and onion rings.
